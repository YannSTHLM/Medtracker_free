MedTracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data Collection

MedTracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All medication information, dose logs, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Room database and SharedPreferences).

Internet Access

This app does not require or use internet access. No data is ever sent to external servers, third parties, or analytics services.

Permissions

MedTracker requests the following permissions solely for local functionality:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To display medication reminder notifications
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — To schedule precise reminder alarms
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To reschedule alarms after device restart
  • VIBRATE — For notification vibration
  • WAKE_LOCK — To wake the device for alarm reminders

Third-Party Services

This app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

All app data can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the application via your device's Settings.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the current policy.

Contact

If you have questions, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
